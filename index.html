Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as 1 task?
Each task is estimated based on complexity and time required. Simple tasks like grocery pickup count as 1 task, while complex projects like garage organization may count as multiple tasks.
How accurate are the time estimates?
Our time estimates are based on industry averages and may vary depending on the specific requirements of your home and family needs.
Can I customize my plan?
Yes! We offer custom plans for families with unique needs. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
